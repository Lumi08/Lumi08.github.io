Project Overview

For my Level 5 Further Games and Graphical Concepts we were tasked to make a scene/game using Direct X 11. For my project I made a demonstration of how my application could load in different models and texture and apply lighting to them correctly. The project allowed me to extend my knowledge of 3D games and allowed me to develop a further understanding in creating 3D games.

Learning Direct X taught me alot about the graphics pipeline and also showed me how shaders are used and how a per pixel shader and vertex shader worked. I enjoyed the direct X project as I found it much more challenging and I had to learn everything from scratch.
