About Me

Hey, I'm Ryan and I like to play games, some of my favourite games include, Terraria, Counter Strike, Osu!, Escape From Tarkov, Minecraft, The Division, and Metal: Hellsinger (Even though ive only gotten hands on the demo so far). From a young age I've always enjoyed mathematics and problem solving then when being introduced into programming I loved it, the satisfaction you get from creating something or overcoming a problem is unmatched. With my passion for games and the new found passion for programming I started learning how games were made and ultimately applied to Staffordshire University for Computer Games Programming.

I'm a very big believer that if you put your mind to something with enough time and effort you will succeed, an example of this in my own experience is art, as a more logical minded individual I always thought it would be impossible for me to learn to draw, however I have overcome this mindset and drew my own twitch emotes for my channel. I personally get so much satisfaction from learning new skills and continue to do so in my free time.
